Kothari International School

campus life

Swimming Pool

Kothari International School has a variety of sport fields to encourage specialised learning in various outdoor activities. In addition there is a spacious multi-purpose hall and swimming pool as well as a fully-equipped gymnasium.


dining hall

Meals are served on campus. The best quality of milk and vegetables are used to prepare vegetarian meals in hygienic kitchens of the school cafeteria. The cafeteria menu offers no junk food but wholesome meals including green vegetables as advised by the School Dietician.

library

A well-resourced library enables students to enrich and expand their horizons. The KIS library has a vast and diverse collection of books for children of all levels. It is constantly expanding its resource centre stocked with books, journals, Computer Systems, videos, DVD’s.

what's new

  • Art / Craft Center

    Kothari International School believes in bringing out the creative best in its pupils. The well-equipped Art and Craft Centre contains a variety of art and music equipment, as well as facilities for dance and drama. Children are encouraged to develop their skills and talents from an early age.

  • Medical services

    The school has a medical room, with a visiting doctor and a team of trained nurses. Regular eye/dental and general health records of all students are maintained. We have agreement with good hospitals to provide prompt attention in the event of medical emergencies.

  • Day Care

    The Day and Creche facility at Kothari Starz provides a home-like environment for children availing the facility. High-quality infrastructure and qualified, trained and friendly professionals keep the children occupied for the entire time that they spend here. While the parents are occupied at work they can rest assured that their children are in safe hands. We mail weekly newsletters to parents regarding the weekly curriculum.

  • ATAL TINKERING LAB

    It was a proud and an auspicious occasion for the staff and the students of Kothari International school on Saturday, March 24, 2018 as they participated in the Inauguration Ceremony of the coveted Atal Tinkering Lab. The Lab was inaugurated by Mr. V. k Saraswat, Padmabhushan and Padmshree, NeetiAayog, by cutting the ribbon and offering the coconut in time honored tradition. The occasion was graced by great celebrities, Mr. Ajay Jadeja and MrShammiNarang. The Lab is equipped with “Do It Yourself Kit” related to science. The 3-D printer was the main attraction of the Lab in addition to devices like Cordless Drill, Heat Gun, Ardino and Rasberrypi, Drone, Four wheel Robotic Kit, Biomedical and Biotech kit, Water Sanitation Kit, and Agritech The lab will boost the scientific innovations and creative ideas in the young minds of the KIS.The school sincerely aims to make all efforts to create innovations to make skilled India. This Lab is definitely going to motivate students to experiment, satisfy their curiosity and encourage adaptive learning and physical computing to understand the basic concepts behind the phenomena to be studied. The event culminated with the interaction between MrSarswat and the students. The students shared their vision and dream for developed and established India.
